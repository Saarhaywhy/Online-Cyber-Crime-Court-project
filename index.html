Cyber Laws
CCC Core Value
Confidentiality

This is part of our organization's efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your data. To do this, access to information is restricted to avoid the purposeful or unintentional sharing of data with unauthorized parties.


Integrity

Integrity requires ensuring that your data is reliable and unaltered. The reliability, accuracy, and validity of the supplied data serve as the foundation for maintaining the integrity of your data.


Availability

To ensure the seamless operation of the application, our systems, networks, and apps must all be operating as they should and at the appropriate times.
