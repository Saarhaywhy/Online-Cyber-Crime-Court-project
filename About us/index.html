About us

CCC is a project carried out by Ayomide Olabisi (Saar_haywhy) an undergraduate student at the Federal University Of Technology, Akure. CCC is refered to as Cyber Crime Court, an online court where Cyber crimes can be reported and also an avenue to sensitize victims and also create awareness on Cyber crimes, cyber law. In addition, News on reported cases and international and local cyber crime news would be displayed on the website.

Our Goal

Our goals just as stated in our About Us section are;

  1. To create awareness on cyber crimes, cyber Laws.
  2. To give progress report on each crime reported and follow it till the end.
  3. To give expert advise on how to combat cyber crime
  4. To update people about various cyber crime happening within and outside of Nigeria.
